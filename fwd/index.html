Project 2 reflection

Forward - I placed my prototype for Milestone 2 in the project section of this site. I really enjoyed this project. My original asperations for this idea were pretty high, and at first when I saw my finished product, I was a little unsatisfied with the results. But after I started working on the UI, I started having a lot of fun exploring Mars. The concept of just strolling through thousands of martian photos ended up being very satisfiying. The API that I used was a little difficult to use, there was no way to requst images from all 5 cameras. Instead I needed to use 5 different requests to get 5 different sets of photos. Parsing the photos was my second challenge, I had some hours where all 5 photos were the same image and I had to do some in depth debugging to figure out what I had messed up when I added more perspectives. But in the end, I enjoyed the project and feel that I did end up learning a lot from the experiance.

Milestone 2 - Portal

The theme of my portal is a sleek modernist design, with a sleek, elegant coloring. Or originally, I considered doing a retro site, with an 80's theme. It would have had a predominantly black background with bright reds, blues, greens, and oranges. In the end, I couldn’t capture the real feeling of a retro website, without it looking poor. I couldn’t solve that design roadblock, so I went around it and created a new sleek design instead. I also had a lot of difficulty with making the page fade in. The fade works well in some browsers, while others may not work on the first run of the page. I didn’t use many resources in my portal, I used jQuery to make the page opacity increase then used it to fade in the page. One thing I really like about this is that I have this structure in my HTML that allows me to know how the CSS and JS will intersect with the page, I just change a couple of classes and my post will continue to be properly formatted. Next time, I will start with more sketching, since I spent a lot of time working on the retro design scheme, and if I had a firmer idea of what I wanted, I could better use my time.

Milestone 1 - Portal

The concept of my portal is a simple and easy gold, black, and white color combination. I wanted something more than a completly minamalist design, so I designed a line to zig zag down the page as more and more posts are created. The page is designed to be easy to understand, with only one axis of movement. It is made useing reactive design, so it will shrink to accomidate smaller screens, and grow to an extent for large devices. The design is modular, so that I can add pictures and display lab assignments easily with only a couple of classes.

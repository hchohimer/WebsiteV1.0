The World on Fire

For those who just want to see it burn

Scroll accross the map to see

All of the major fires spreading around the globe right now. These fires are cataloged by the Earth Observatory Natural Event Tracker. A Program run by NASA which uses satalite data to catalog and record major global events. This display data is the current catalog of their "open" fires. Fires which are still ongoing in some aspect. The numbers displayed accross the map are air quality measurments compiled by the EPA and made API accessible by the World Air Quality Index Project.

created by Hudson Hohimer

Data supplied by EONET at https://eonet.sci.gsfc.nasa.gov/ and air quality data supplied by the World Air Quality Index Project and the EPA.
